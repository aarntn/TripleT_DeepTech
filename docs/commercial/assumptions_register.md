# SolarGuard / SolarSense: Evidence Quality / Assumptions Register

**Purpose:** Judge and investor audit trail. Each row maps a commercial or technical claim to its evidence base, confidence level, and the specific risk that could invalidate it.

**Last updated:** 2026-05-15 — revised against hackathon preparation document (deeptech hack v4)

**Evidence levels:**
- **Strong** — multiple independent peer-reviewed / official sources with quantitative agreement, or single local field study directly measuring the claimed figure
- **Medium-strong** — 2+ sources with directional agreement; one or more caveats
- **Medium** — limited or indirect sources; plausible but not yet directly validated
- **Weak** — internal model assumption; external data found to be in tension with the claim
